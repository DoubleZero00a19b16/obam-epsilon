## ğŸš€ The Problem: "Data Blindness"
OBA Markets is a retail giant, but it faces a silent problem common in the "Hard Discount" sector:
1.  **Private Label Risk:** When an OBA-brand product fails, the company doesn't know *why* (Taste? Packaging? Price?). This leads to millions in wasted inventory.
2.  **The "Silent Majority" Gap:** Retailers only hear from customers when they are angry. There is zero data on what customers love, making it hard to identify "Winning Products."
3.  **Export Uncertainty:** Without concrete data on local popularity, it is difficult to convince foreign distributors (in Georgia, Russia, etc.) to import OBA products.

## ğŸ’¡ The Solution: Incentivized Product Intelligence
**OBA Rate & Earn** is a mobile integration that transforms the loyalty app into a **Crowdsourced R&D Department**.

We incentivize customers to rate products after purchase. By gamifying the feedback loop, we gather "Healthy Data" that allows OBA to:
*   **Optimize Manufacturing:** Know exactly why a product is rated 2 stars (e.g., "Too salty") and fix the recipe immediately.
*   **Power Export Strategy:** Use 5-star rating data as proof of quality to enter international markets.
*   **Predict Demand:** Identify viral products before they sell out.
